   - Need ~10,000 views for 1 conversion (0.01% rate)

   - People don't buy from accounts they just discovered
   - Need multiple touchpoints
   - Brand awareness takes time
   - First 3 days = awareness phase, not sales phase

   - Content violates guidelines
   - Coordinated behavior flagged

   - Videos don't provide value

   - Low production quality
   - Boring or repetitive

3. **No Market Fit (20% of failures)**

   - Target audience not interested

   - Better alternatives exist

4. **Lack of Patience (15% of failures)**
   - Giving up after 1-2 weeks
   - Expecting instant results
   - Not optimizing based on data
   - Inconsistent posting

5. **Technical Issues (10% of failures)**
   - Automation breaks
   - Accounts get locked
   - Videos fail to upload
   - System maintenance
